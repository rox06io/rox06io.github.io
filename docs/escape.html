Escape!

Cardboard app


Description

In a distant village, there is a lonely house. It is said that one day a widow woman lived and died because of love. In their free-time, this house is the favorite entertainment for the children, but anyone who steps inside is unable to leave. Are you allowed to leave?

Use your VR cardboard headset to try to escape the house.
