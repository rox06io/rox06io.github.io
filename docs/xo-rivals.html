XO Rivals

Unattended multiplayer


Description

The cities of Tricius have been running events for years in order to decide who will be the winner of the annual world's games. The semifinal confronts Circulus and Crucius to see who will govern the planet.

You'll have to put yourself in the shoes of one of these cities and get the victory.

XO Rivals is an unattended multiplayer, your goal will be to win the tic-tac-toe to beat the other city. However, attaining victory will not be so simple. You will have to win all the minigames to be able to place your pieces. You will also have to choose your opponent's minigame to impede them from putting their piece.

All I can do is wish you luck...
