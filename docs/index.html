About me

What could I tell you about me?

In 2018, I started my degree in Madrid Game Development and Design, where I found my role in the video game industry: Game Design.

My degree partners and I took a risk and made games together under the name Virtual Ravens Games, and I discovered I had a true passion for UI/UX Design as well as Game Design. Having the ability to empathize with users and anticipate their needs is one of my favorite things about this job.
My experience with Virtual Ravens exposed me to working as a team and leading it (as well as trying the roles of producer and artist). When I'm not brainstorming new ideas, I like to watch series or spend time with my family.

Additionally, I speak Spanish natively and have a B2 in English .
